Job Description

(
Team Lead
)

As a Team Lead - Operations, you’ll oversee day-to-day operations, ensuring that your team consistently meets performance goals while providing outstanding service to our global clients. You’ll play a critical role in shaping team culture, improving processes, and driving success in delivering high-quality BPO services.

This is a full-time leadership position offering exciting challenges and opportunities for career growth in an international-facing environment.

Job Responsibilities

  • Team Management: Supervise, mentor, and motivate a team of operations staff to achieve individual and team goals.
  • Performance Oversight: Monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), ensuring targets are met or exceeded.
  • Process Improvement: Identify operational inefficiencies and implement strategies to optimize workflows and enhance productivity.
  • Quality Assurance: Ensure the team delivers high-quality work that aligns with client expectations and company standards.
  • Training & Development: Provide coaching, feedback, and training to team members to enhance their skills and career growth.
  • Client Coordination: Serve as a point of contact for clients, ensuring excellent communication and satisfaction with service delivery.
  • Reporting: Prepare and present performance reports, including metrics, achievements, and areas for improvement, to senior management.

What We’re Looking For

  • Experience:
    - 3+ years of experience in a BPO or call center environment, with at least 1 year in a supervisory or team lead role.
    - Proven track record of managing teams and delivering results in a fast-paced environment.
  • Skills:
    - Exceptional leadership and interpersonal skills to inspire and guide a team.
    - Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities to identify and resolve operational challenges.
    - Excellent communication skills in both Amharic & English (both written and verbal).
    - Proficiency in using CRM, workforce management, and reporting tools.
  • Education: A bachelor’s degree in business management, operations, or a related field is preferred.
  • Location: Based in Addis Ababa with flexibility for hybrid or in-office work as needed.

Employee Benefits

  • Global Opportunities: Work with international clients and gain global exposure.
  • Career Growth: Be part of a leading BPO company with opportunities for advancement.
  • Leadership Role: Take on a pivotal role in a growing company where your contributions make a direct impact.
  • Supportive Environment: Join a company that values your contributions and invests in your professional development.
  • Competitive Compensation: We offer a competitive salary and benefits package.
