Job Description

(
Customer Support
)

As a Customer Support Specialist, you’ll be the voice of our clients’ businesses, helping customers resolve their inquiries and providing an outstanding customer experience. You’ll work with a diverse team, using your communication skills and problem-solving abilities to make a real impact on customers around the world.

If you thrive in a fast-paced environment, have a passion for delivering top-notch service, and want to work with global clients, this is the perfect opportunity for you!

Job Responsibilities

This is a full-time position offering the chance to grow your skills in a fast-paced, client-centric environment.

  • Customer Assistance: Respond to customer inquiries via email, chat, and phone, ensuring prompt and professional communication.
  • Problem Resolution: Address and resolve customer concerns with empathy and efficiency, escalating issues when necessary.
  • Order & Account Management: Assist customers with placing orders, tracking shipments, and updating account information.
  • Knowledge Sharing: Stay up-to-date with client products, services, and policies to deliver accurate and informed support.
  • Feedback Collection: Gather insights from customers and provide recommendations to improve processes and services.
  • Team Collaboration: Work closely with team members and supervisors to meet performance goals and client expectations.

What We’re Looking For

  • Experience:
    - At least 1-2 years in a customer support, call center, or BPO environment.
    - Experience working with international clients is a plus.
  • Skills:
    - Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English (additional languages are a bonus).
    - Strong active listening and problem-solving abilities.
    - Proficiency in using CRM tools, email platforms, and customer support software.
    - Ability to multitask, prioritize, and remain calm under pressure.
  • Education: Bachelor’s degree in business, communications, or a related field is preferred.
  • Location: Based in Addis Ababa with reliable internet for remote or hybrid work, if required.

Employee Benefits

  • Global Opportunities: Work with international clients and gain global exposure.
  • Career Growth: Be part of a leading BPO company with opportunities for advancement.
  • Supportive Environment: Join a company that values your contributions and invests in your professional development.
  • Competitive Compensation: We offer a competitive salary and benefits package.
